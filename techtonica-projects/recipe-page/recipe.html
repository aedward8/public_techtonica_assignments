Avocado Toast Recipe!

A classic recipe but can easily be sold for $12 in a cafe in San Francisco. For this reason it is highly favorable to learn to make this at home

photo credit:Macheesmo Food Blog

Ingredients List

  • 2 pieces of multigrain bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • black pepper and salt for seasoning
  • optional chili flakes or coconut aminos

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in the pan
  2. Once the oil is hot crack both eggs into the pan to make sunny side eggs
  3. As eggs are cooking in the pan put both slices of bread into the toaster
  4. set time on toaster to desired toast level
  5. Cut the avocado in the middle
  6. Take out the 2 toasted pieces of bread and put on flat plate
  7. Scoop 1/4 of avocado onto one toast and spread evenly
  8. Scoop out another 1/4 of avocado and spread evenly on 2nd toast
  9. Store the rest of the avocado in fridge
  10. Turn off head and put one sunny side up egg on each toast
  11. Season to your liking with salt,pepper, and any other ingredients
  12. Ready to eat and serve
